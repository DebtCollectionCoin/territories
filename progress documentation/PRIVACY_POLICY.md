# Privacy policy — Territories

_Last updated: May 2026._

Territories ("the app") is an offline two-player board game.
The app does not collect, transmit, store on remote servers, sell, or
share any personal information.

## Data we collect

**None.** We do not collect:

- personally identifying information,
- account credentials,
- contacts, location, calendar, or sensors data,
- analytics, telemetry, or crash reports,
- advertising identifiers.

## Data stored on your device

The app keeps the following on your device only, and never transmits
it off-device:

- Game configuration choices (board size, scoring variant, opponent
  difficulty).
- Saved games (the moves you have played in finished and in-progress
  games), so you can review history and resume a game after closing
  the app.
- UI preferences (sound, haptics, colour-blind mode toggles).

This data lives in the app's private storage (Android Room database
and `SharedPreferences`). It is included in standard Android backups
unless you have disabled them in Settings.

You can delete all of it at any time via:

- **Settings → Apps → Territories → Storage → Clear data**, or
- uninstalling the app.

## Permissions

The app requests no runtime permissions. It does not access the network.

## Children

The app is suitable for all ages. Because we do not collect any data,
the app does not target advertising or content based on age, identity,
or location.

## Third-party services

The app uses **no third-party SDKs**, analytics frameworks, ad networks,
crash reporters, or trackers.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated text will replace this file in
[the project repository](https://github.com/DebtCollectionCoin/territories).
The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the current version.
